Sauteedmushrooms

Description

These sauteed mushrooms are the quintessential topping for all types of steaks as well as baked potatoes and they are delicious!

Ingrediants

  • Oil and butter: For the best and richest flavor, cook the mushrooms in a mixture of olive oil and butter
  • mushrooms: A pound of sliced buttom mushrooms should serve about four people
  • wine: A tablespoon of red cooking wine enhances the flavor the mushrooms
  • Teriyaki sauce: use store bought or home made
  • Garlic: fresh garlic gives a bold flavor
  • Seasonings: these sauteed mushrooms are simply seasoned with garlic powder and black pepper. you can also add salt but the teriyaki sauce should be salty enough

Steps

  1. Heat the fat
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and brown the mushrooms
  3. Simmer until the mushrooms are tender
